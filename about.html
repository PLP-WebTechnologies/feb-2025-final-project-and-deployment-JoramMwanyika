About Sunsail Hotel

Discover our story and the authentic Lamu experience

Our Story

A Heritage of Hospitality

Sunsail Hotel has been welcoming guests to the enchanting island of Lamu for over two decades. Established in 1998, our hotel was born from a vision to create an authentic Swahili experience that honors the rich cultural heritage of Lamu while providing modern comforts for travelers.

The building itself is a testament to traditional Swahili architecture, featuring thick coral stone walls, intricate wooden doors, and spacious verandahs that catch the cooling ocean breezes. Originally a merchant's home from the 19th century, the structure has been carefully restored and adapted to serve as a boutique hotel while preserving its historical character.

Over the years, Sunsail Hotel has become a beloved landmark on Lamu's waterfront, known for its warm hospitality, delicious cuisine, and authentic cultural experience. We take pride in offering our guests a genuine taste of Lamu's unique way of life, where time slows down and traditions are cherished.

The Lamu Experience

Discover the Magic of Lamu Island

Lamu Island is a place where time seems to stand still. As one of the oldest and best-preserved Swahili settlements in East Africa, Lamu Town has been designated a UNESCO World Heritage site for its outstanding cultural significance.

The island has no cars – transportation is by foot, donkey, or traditional dhow boats. This creates a peaceful atmosphere where you can truly disconnect from the hustle and bustle of modern life and immerse yourself in a different rhythm.

At Sunsail Hotel, we help our guests experience the best of what Lamu has to offer. From exploring the narrow winding streets of Lamu Town with its beautiful architecture, to sailing on a traditional dhow at sunset, to enjoying fresh seafood caught daily by local fishermen – there are countless ways to discover the island's unique charm.

Our staff, many of whom are Lamu natives, are always happy to share their knowledge and recommendations to help you create your perfect Lamu experience.
