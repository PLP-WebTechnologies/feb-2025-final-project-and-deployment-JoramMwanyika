Deluxe Room

Spacious accommodation with traditional Swahili elegance

Room Description

Our Deluxe Room offers a perfect blend of traditional Swahili design and modern comfort. The spacious room features a beautiful king-size four-poster bed with mosquito netting, handcrafted wooden furniture, and a private balcony with stunning views of the Indian Ocean.

The room is decorated with authentic Lamu artifacts and textiles, creating a warm and culturally rich atmosphere. High ceilings with traditional wooden beams and large windows allow for natural light and cooling sea breezes.

Each Deluxe Room has a private en-suite bathroom with hot and cold water, complimentary toiletries, and plush towels. The room is equipped with modern amenities while maintaining the authentic charm of Lamu's architectural heritage.
