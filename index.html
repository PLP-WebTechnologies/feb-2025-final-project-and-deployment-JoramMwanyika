About Us

A Piece of Paradise in Lamu

Sunsail Hotel has been welcoming guests to Lamu Island for over two decades, offering an authentic coastal Kenyan experience with our warm hospitality and beautiful surroundings.

Our hotel features traditional Swahili architecture with modern amenities, creating a comfortable and culturally rich environment for our guests. The spacious verandahs, intricate woodwork, and airy rooms reflect the unique heritage of Lamu Island.

Located directly on the waterfront, we offer easy access to Lamu Town's historic sites, dhow cruises, and pristine beaches. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, Sunsail Hotel provides the perfect base for your Lamu experience.
